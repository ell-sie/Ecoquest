Empowering Rwandan Communities for Sustainable Coexistence

Nestled in the heart of Rwanda, a land of vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes, a unique bond between rural communities and wildlife has flourished for generations. Across the rolling hills and lush valleys, people have woven their lives with the rhythm of nature. Today, this bond faces a new challenge, as global dynamics intersect with local traditions. Proposed legislation from afar threatens to disrupt the delicate tapestry of coexistence that Rwandans hold dear.
Our advocacy campaign is a rallying call, echoing through the hills and valleys, as we stand together to safeguard the rights of Rwandan communities. These custodians of nature are the backbone of sustainable progress, nurturing both their heritage and the diverse wildlife that shares their land.

Beyond the Surface:The True Essence of Conservation

In the weave of this intricate connection lies responsible hunting, a practice that goes beyond the surface and defines true conservation. Far from mere trophies, this tradition fuels a cycle of empowerment, echoing through the lives of those who rely on it. With deep respect for nature's gifts, every part of the hunted animal finds purpose, from sustenance to community development projects that fortify the fabric of society.

A Threat Across Oceans

As waves of policy discussions ripple across oceans, the future of Rwandan conservancies hangs in the balance. Proposed bans on hunting trophies could inadvertently sever the lifeline of communities, impacting economies, jobs, and the very essence of coexistence. A call to action echoes, not only for Rwandans but for all who cherish the delicate dance of life shared between humans and wildlife.

Harmonizing Progress, Tradition, and Women's Empowerment

Our journey is one of harmony – a harmony that finds its rhythm in the coexistence of tradition, progress, and women's empowerment. By championing the voices of Rwandan communities, particularly the resilient women who lead them, we strive to ensure a future where the land thrives, communities flourish, women are empowered, and the rich tapestry of culture and nature remains intact.

who Is Leading This Advocacy Campaign?

This advocacy campaign is being led by a consortium of organisations working to support and protect the right of African communities to participate in, control, manage and sustainably use their natural resources including wildlife. The organisations include the Community Leaders Network (CLN) of Southern Africa, which represents over eight million rural people managing conservancies across the region, Resource Africa Southern Africa and Resource Africa UK. The campaign was launched in December 2021.

Voices of Change

This advocacy movement is led by passionate individuals and organizations rooted in Rwanda, united under the banner of sustainable coexistence and women's empowerment. Together, we amplify the voices that bridge tradition, progress, and women's empowerment, echoing the aspirations of a nation that seeks a brighter future while honoring its past.
